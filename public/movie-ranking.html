The Best Movies According to John

My Top 3 Movies

Apocalypto

This is my all time favourite movie. From the beggining to the end there is no boring scene or any part that is not entertaining. This is the movie that I have repeatedly watched the most

Peaky Blinders

I like the brotherhood and how the family supported eachother through thin and thick. In this 60 episodes series devided into 6 seasons, I like the wisdom and leadership of Thomas who was fearless, brave and a family man who supported his family and friends.

Luca

This is my favourate anime movie. I like the friendship and how Alberto supported Luca and welcomed him to the family. Although he was just a sea monster but Luca was able to learn, live with humans and fulfil his dream of riding a Vespa
